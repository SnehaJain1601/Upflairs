Favourite Hobby

Spending Time with Friends

Spending time with friends is more than just a hobby for me. It is a source of happiness, emotional strength, and lifelong memories. In a world that often feels rushed and demanding, being with friends provides a comforting escape from the routine pressures of life. Whenever I am surrounded by my close friends, I feel a sense of belonging and warmth that cannot be replaced by anything else. We laugh together, share our thoughts freely, and support each other through every phase of life. These moments, no matter how simple they seem, become deeply meaningful.



Our time together is never just about having fun—though we have plenty of that—it’s also about connection, understanding, and shared growth. We go on spontaneous outings, watch movies, play games, have late-night talks, and celebrate even the smallest victories. Each activity, whether it's a long conversation or a short walk, becomes memorable when done with true friends. They have seen me at my best and stood by me at my worst, and that loyalty makes every moment we spend together feel special. What makes this hobby even more beautiful is that it doesn’t require anything fancy or expensive. Sometimes, sitting under the stars and talking for hours means more than any big party or outing. The genuine bond and laughter we share are what I treasure the most. These friendships have taught me the value of trust, empathy, and companionship, which are essential in all aspects of life. Early Life

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalamwas born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He came from a humble background and was raised in a simple, disciplined household. Despite financial difficulties, he was always a dedicated student and had a deep interest in science and mathematics.

Achievements

Dr. Kalam played a key role in India's missile development program and was closely associated with the Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998. He was known as the “Missile Man of India” for his work on ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. He later served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007 and inspired millions through his humility, wisdom, and dedication.

Inspiration

Dr. Kalam was a great teacher at heart. He believed in the power of dreams, hard work, and education. His books like "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds" have inspired the youth of India. His life is a perfect example of how determination and vision can transform a person’s destiny and also that of a nation.

My Daily Routine

Morning

I start my day early with a refreshing cup of coffee and a short meditation session to clear my mind. After that, I go for a quick jog around the neighborhood. Once I’m back, I take a shower and have a healthy breakfast to get energized for the day ahead.

Afternoon

In the afternoon, I focus on my work or studies. I spend a few hours working on tasks, attending meetings, or learning something new. Around lunchtime, I take a break to enjoy a balanced meal and relax for a bit before continuing with my work.

Evening

In the evening, I like to unwind by spending time with my family or friends. I usually go for a walk or watch a movie. After dinner, I read a book or listen to music to relax. Before going to bed, I plan my next day and ensure I get a good night’s sleep.
